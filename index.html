Yes, completely. Capital One Shopping is free to use and always will be. No premium tier, no hidden fees, no credit card required. Capital One earns a small commission from merchants when it saves you money — so the better it works for you, the better it works for everyone.

Do I need a Capital One credit card or bank account? +

No. Capital One Shopping works for anyone with a browser, regardless of what bank or credit cards you use. You don't need to be a Capital One customer at all.

Is my personal or financial data safe? +

Yes. The extension only activates on retail and travel shopping sites. It does not access your banking information, passwords, or sensitive personal data. You can review the full privacy policy at any time and remove it in one click.

Yes. It works on Expedia, VRBO, Priceline, Booking.com, IHG, Hilton, and many more travel sites where you can earn cashback on eligible purchases.
